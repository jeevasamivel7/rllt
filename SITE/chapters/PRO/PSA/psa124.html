Psalms 124

The Lord the Defense of His People

A Song of Ascents. Of David.

1"If it had not been the Lord who was on our side,"Let Israel now say-

2"If it had not been the Lord who was on our side,When men rose up against us,

3Then they would have swallowed us alive,When their wrath was kindled against us;

4Then the waters would have overwhelmed us,The stream would have gone over our soul;

5Then the swollen waters Would have gone over our soul."

6Blessed be the Lord,Who has not given us as prey to their teeth.

7Our soul has escaped as a bird from the snare of the fowlers;The snare is broken, and we have escaped.

8Our help is in the name of the Lord,Who made heaven and earth.
