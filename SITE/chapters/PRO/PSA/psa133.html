Psalms 133

Blessed Unity of the People of God

A Song of Ascents. Of David.

1Behold, how good and how pleasant it is For brethren to dwell together in unity!

2It is like the precious oil upon the head,Running down on the beard,The beard of Aaron,Running down on the edge of his garments.

3It is like the dew of Hermon,Descending upon the mountains of Zion;For there the Lord commanded the blessing—Life forevermore.
