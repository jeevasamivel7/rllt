Psalms 126

A Joyful Return to Zion

A Song of Ascents.

1When the Lord brought back the captivity of Zion,We were like those who dream.

2Then our mouth was filled with laughter,And our tongue with singing.Then they said among the nations,�The Lord has done great things for them.�

3The Lord has done great things for us,And we are glad.

4Bring back our captivity, O Lord,As the streams in the South.

5Those who sow in tears Shall reap in joy.

6He who continually goes forth weeping,Bearing seed for sowing,Shall doubtless come again with rejoicing,Bringing his sheaves with him.
