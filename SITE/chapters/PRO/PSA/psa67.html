Psalms 67

An Invocation and a Doxology

To the Chief Musician. On stringed instruments. A Psalm. A Song.

1God be merciful to us and bless us,And cause His face to shine upon us, Selah

2That Your way may be known on earth,Your salvation among all nations.

3Let the peoples praise You, O God;Let all the peoples praise You.

4Oh, let the nations be glad and sing for joy!For You shall judge the people righteously,And govern the nations on earth. Selah

5Let the peoples praise You, O God;Let all the peoples praise You.

6Then the earth shall yield her increase;God, our own God, shall bless us.

7God shall bless us,And all the ends of the earth shall fear Him.
