Psalms 93

The Eternal Reign of the Lord

1The Lord reigns, He is clothed with majesty;The Lord is clothed,He has girded Himself with strength.Surely the world is established, so that it cannot be moved.

2Your throne is established from of old;You are from everlasting.

3The floods have lifted up, O Lord,The floods have lifted up their voice;The floods lift up their waves.

4The Lord on high is mightier Than the noise of many waters,Than the mighty waves of the sea.

5Your testimonies are very sure;Holiness adorns Your house,O Lord, forever.
